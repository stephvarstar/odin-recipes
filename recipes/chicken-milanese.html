Chicken Milanese

Description

Chicken milanese is delicious protein to add to any dish to add that crispy savory bite. Add it to pasta, salads or whatever you rheart desires. This recipe is simple and only takes 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (95 degrees C)
  2. beat eggs with salt and pepper in a shallow dish. Spread flour in another dish and bread crumbs in a third dish.
  3. Working with one pieve at a time, gently press chicken into flour ot coat and shake off any excess, Dip into beaten eggs, then press into bread crumbs. Gently toss between your hands so excess bread crumbs can fall away. Place breaded chicken onto a plate while breading the rest; do not stack.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Pan fry chicken in batches or 2 or 3 pieces until golden brown and no longer pink in the center, 2 to 4 minutes per side. An instant read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Transfer cooked chicken to a baking sheet and keep warm in the preheated overn while cooking remaining chicken.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges.
