Chicken Bacon Ranch Pasta

Description

If you love pasta and bacon, try this recipe out. It's a go to for an easy weeknight dinner and everyone will love it. It only takes 30 minutes to make this creamy and savory chicken bacon ranch pasta. Just add a side salad and enjoy a cool glass of tea with it.

Ingredients

  • 3 3/4 cups uncooked rotini pasta
  • 4 thick-cut bacon slices
  • 1 lb skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 tbsp dry ranch dressing mix

Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened
  • 5 tsp dry ranch dressing mix
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain rotini, saving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Meanwhile, place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crispy, about 10 minutes. Remove bacon and drain on paper towels. Leave rendered bacon grease in the skillet.
  3. Sprinkle chicken pieces with 1 tablespoon of ranch seasoning. Add tot he skilled with the bacon grease and cook until golden brown, 5-8 minutes. Remove cooked chicken from skillet and set aside.
  4. Rinse out skillet. Melt down 2 tablespoons of butter in teh skillet over medium heat. Whisk in flour until smooth and thickened, about 2 minutes. Slowly add in milk, whisk until sauce is smooth. Add pasta water, 1 tablespooon at a time, if the sauce is too thick.
  5. Stir softened cream cheese into sauce until well combined. Mix in remaining 5 teaspoons of ranch seasoning and season with salt. Mix until well combined. Add cooked pasta and chicken back into the skillet and mix until well coated with sauce.
  6. Finely chop cooked bacon. Mix into pasta dish. Serve garnished with chopped parsley if desired.
